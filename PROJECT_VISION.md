# PrayerRise

## Mission
Help Muslims stay connected to Allah through prayer, remembrance, reflection, discipline, and peaceful routines.

---

## Core Features

### Prayer System
- Accurate prayer times
- Adhan alarms
- Smart wake-up
- Anti-snooze

### Spiritual Guidance
- Tafsir after prayer
- Hadith reminders
- Dhikr & Azkar
- Quran reflections

### Rahma Mode
Support women spiritually during periods through:
- Dhikr
- Duas
- Reflection
- Comfort reminders
- Reward encouragement

### Planner
- Salah schedules
- Quran goals
- Productivity system
- Ramadan planner
